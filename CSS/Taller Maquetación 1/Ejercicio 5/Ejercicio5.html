Space monsters

Black hole

Black holes are ultra-dense cosmic objects with gravity so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape. They form from collapsed massive stars and create a zone called the event horizon, marking a point of no return.

Neutron Star

Neutron stars are super-dense leftovers from massive star explosions. Packed with neutrons, they're tiny but heavy, emitting strong pulses of radiation.

Pulsar

Pulsars are fast-spinning neutron stars emitting rhythmic beams of radiation.

Quasar

Quasars are distant, incredibly bright objects powered by supermassive black holes at their centers.

Star

Stars are luminous celestial bodies that emit light and heat due to nuclear fusion reactions in their cores.
